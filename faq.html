Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What makes SeldomSought different?

While traditional research services focus on readily available information, we specialize in the obscure, the forgotten, and the deliberately hidden. Our team includes specialists in rare fields with access to restricted archives and relationships with experts worldwide.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple inquiries might be resolved in 24-48 hours, while comprehensive research can take weeks or months. We provide realistic timelines during consultation and maintain transparent communication throughout.

Can you research topics in other languages?

Absolutely. Our network includes researchers fluent in over 40 modern languages and specialists in ancient languages including Latin, Ancient Greek, Sanskrit, Classical Chinese, Aramaic, and many others.

What if my question seems impossible to answer?

Obscure questions are our specialty. While we can't guarantee answers to every question, we have a 95% success rate even with seemingly impossible inquiries. We'll provide an honest assessment and explore creative approaches to finding answers.

How do you ensure research accuracy?

Every finding undergoes rigorous verification. We cross-reference multiple primary sources, consult subject matter experts, and employ peer review processes. All research includes detailed citations and methodology documentation.

Do you maintain confidentiality?

Absolutely. We maintain strict confidentiality for all client projects. Non-disclosure agreements are available upon request. Our researchers are bound by professional ethics and employ secure communication channels.
