Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers about what we do and how we work

What kind of clients do you work with?

Businesses and founders who are serious about their brand — whether they're launching, repositioning, or trying to scale. We work well with people who have a strong product and need the marketing and image to match it.

What does a typical engagement look like?

Most engagements combine strategy with execution. That might mean brand image development plus a copywriting package, SEO and AEO setup, or a guerrilla marketing campaign paired with email and SMS sequences. We scope based on what you actually need.

What is AEO and why does it matter?

Answer Engine Optimization is about making your brand show up when people ask AI tools, voice assistants, and search engines direct questions. As AI-driven search grows, AEO is becoming as important as traditional SEO — and most businesses are behind on it.

Do you handle both strategy and execution?

Yes. We don't just hand over a brand deck and wish you luck. We write the copy, run the email campaigns, execute the guerrilla marketing, and optimize your search presence. Strategy and execution under one roof.

How does guerrilla marketing work for established brands?

Guerrilla marketing isn't just for scrappy startups. It's about finding unconventional angles that earn disproportionate attention — without requiring a massive ad spend. We design campaigns around your specific audience and what will make them pay attention.

What's the best way to start?

Reach out via the contact form. Tell us what you're working on and where you're stuck. We'll set up a conversation to see if there's a fit. No pressure, no pitch deck.
