On curation

Instagram orders content by time. That's the wrong axis for an account built around a visual eye. Chronological sorting shows you individual moments — it hides what makes a deliberately built feed worth studying: the color story, the gradual drift in tone and mood across hundreds of posts.

Every image here has been placed where it fits the gradient, not when it was taken. Content is subordinate to composition. The grid becomes a single sustained argument about what it means to look at the world with intention.
