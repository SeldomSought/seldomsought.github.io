Module 2 of 2

Forced Choice

Thurstonian Ranking Blocks — 10 triplets

Each block shows three statements. Click the statements in order:

  • 1st click — the statement most like you
  • 2nd click — the statement somewhat like you
  • 3rd click — the statement least like you

You must rank all three before proceeding. To change a ranking, click the statement again to reset the block.

Module 3 of 3

Values & Attachment

Two brief supplemental scales — 16 items total

The next 16 items cover two distinct dimensions not captured by the HEXACO personality profile:

  • Values (10 items) — How much each of 10 core values guides your life. Rated on a 1–5 importance scale. Reveals your motivational hierarchy.
  • Attachment (6 items) — How you tend to relate in close emotional bonds. Produces an attachment orientation (Secure / Preoccupied / Dismissing / Fearful-Avoidant).

Answer honestly based on your actual patterns, not how you wish you were. There are no right or wrong answers.
